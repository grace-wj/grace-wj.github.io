a year of making, drawn as a tide chart

The horizontal axis is the year, January at the left, December at the right. The vertical axis is depth — the surface is daily correspondence and incidental work, the deep is long, slow, single-minded labor. Each work is moored at the date and the depth at which it was made. Scroll right; the tide rises and falls; the pigment shifts with the month.
