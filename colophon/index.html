A printer's colophon is the line at the back of a book that names who set it. WGWJ is the call sign Grace's first concept used. This page is both — a printed colophon shaped like a tuning dial, set to one of seven works at a time. Drag the needle, or scroll over the dial; ±0.3 of any station and you read it.
