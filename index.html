grace wei-li juan

you are in a foyer.

twelve doors line one side. four further on, in a small second wing, borrow parts of the first twelve back. step through any of them; come back when you like.

wing i · twelve originals

each door, one site — weird on its own axis.

i. terminal a unix shell — type to get anywhere ii. archive a 2D pinboard — drag to pan, scroll to zoom iii. marginalia a critical edition — footnotes all the way down iv. signal an FM tuner — WGWJ, 88.0–108.0 v. studio a 3D painter’s room — drag to orbit vi. press a riso zine — six spreads in two colors vii. constellation a knowledge graph — drag a star viii. atelier a sketchbook — pigments are the navigation ix. carillon six bells — strike one, strike a chord x. emblem a procedural emblem book — every visit, one leaf xi. draft a self-portrait, written live — strikethroughs kept xii. passage a handscroll — scroll down, the day passes

wing ii · four remixes

recombined from the parts of the first twelve. each draws from at least three.

xiii. orrery six works orbit a lamp — when two cross, a bell tolls after studio · carillon · constellation · draft xiv. galley a riso zine — prints itself in front of you after press · draft · emblem xv. tideline a year as a tide chart — scroll east, depth ↓ after passage · atelier · archive · signal xvi. warp a year, woven — fifty-two weft rows, three of them red after atelier · draft · marginalia · press

each door is a branch — concept/<name> — pulled into its own folder. wing ii borrows wing i's parts; nothing here is final.
