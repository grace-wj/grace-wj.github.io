DebaterHub 3D Search Interface

A 3D planet UI for navigating database search results — clusters as solar systems, documents as planets.

An interactive platform that displays database search results as 3D planets, allowing users to explore hierarchical levels of related clusters, documents, sections, and passages.

Built with Three.js and GSAP. Result clusters render as orbiting solar systems; drilling into a planet expands its document, sections, and passages in place.
