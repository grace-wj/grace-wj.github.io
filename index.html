grace wei-li juan

you are in a foyer.

twenty doors. the centerpiece is a small press-family of six — where the taste has converged. the rest are kept in two other wings: eleven originals from the first three rounds, and three remixes from the fourth.

centerpiece · press family · six doors in one ink

all six hold the same riso visual frame — fluorescent pink + cornflower blue + warm black on cream. they differ in what the visitor does.

vi. press a riso zine — six spreads in two colors the anchor · the visual frame xiv. galley a riso zine — prints itself in front of you the anchor · medium as performance xvii. forme a printer’s forme of locked-up type — drag to rotate 3D drag · held flat in css 3d xviii. imprint a constellation of works — riso-printed, drag a node node-and-edge · verbs in mono xix. colophon a printed tuning dial — ±0.3 of any station and you read it signal’s dial · restyled in ink xx. quire eight stitched leaves — scroll ↓ to pan east horizontal scroll · vertical book

wing · eleven originals

each door, one site — weird on its own axis. press has moved upstairs into the centerpiece.

i. terminal a unix shell — type to get anywhere ii. archive a 2D pinboard — drag to pan, scroll to zoom iii. marginalia a critical edition — footnotes all the way down iv. signal an FM tuner — WGWJ, 88.0–108.0 v. studio a 3D painter’s room — drag to orbit vii. constellation a knowledge graph — drag a star viii. atelier a sketchbook — pigments are the navigation ix. carillon six bells — strike one, strike a chord x. emblem a procedural emblem book — every visit, one leaf xi. draft a self-portrait, written live — strikethroughs kept xii. passage a handscroll — scroll down, the day passes

wing · three remixes

recombined from the parts of the originals. each draws from at least three. galley has moved upstairs into the centerpiece.
