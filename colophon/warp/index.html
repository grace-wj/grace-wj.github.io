a year, woven

Fifty-two weft rows, one for each week of the year. The pigment shifts with the month — January in lead white, July in raw sienna, December in french ultramarine. weft is one of Grace's projects; this page is what it produces. Hover a row for the margin note; some weeks are silent.

hover a row. Each weft is one week of making. The width of a row is the depth of the work; the colour is what it was made in.
