Jay-Zam!

A Shazam-like audio-fingerprinting app, scoped to Jay-Z's discography.

A full-stack song-identification app modeled after Shazam. Records audio from the browser, fingerprints it, and matches it against a Jay-Z song database — limited to one artist to keep the local index small.

Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on the front-end; Python and Flask for the API; Docker for packaging. Audio classification uses spectrogram peak hashing.
